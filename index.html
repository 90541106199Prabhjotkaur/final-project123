About Us

The Rubicon comes with a 1-inch lift over the Wrangler Sport and Sahara, and this one is another 2 inches higher than that. There are not any low step rails, either, as that would defeat the purpose of having a lifted Wrangler (that is, better ground clearance for off-road obstacles). The upside is that you can roll over just about anything, and the higher seating position helps you see over surrounding traffic.
